But not yet. The sun’s dying rays paint the rolling hills. It’s quite beautiful. WE PAN OVER TO THE BASKETBALL COURT now where a ball lies in the grass beneath the hoop. Jack approaches. Slips his hands out of his sweatshirt pouch and lifts the ball. He carries it onto the court, flips it out and shoots -- swish! Retrieves the ball, flips it out again, shoots -- swish! As this continues, we very slowly -- FADE OUT.
