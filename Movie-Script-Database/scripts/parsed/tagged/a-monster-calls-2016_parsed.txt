Pages and pages of the Monster. Conor stops on one, a good one, the Monster looking ominous, powerful. On the desk, pencils start to ROLL slightly towards the sketchpad. ANGLE ON: Conor looking at the page, a small BREEZE stirring his hair, the slightest hint of a private smile curling up. We pull back, giving him his privacy and we: FADE TO BLACK.
